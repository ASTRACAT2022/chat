Project Summary

This project, "AppleTechOverview," is a curated collection of information detailing various Apple product lines. It aims to provide a clear and accessible overview of the iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch, highlighting their key features, innovations, and impact. Each section is designed to be informative and visually engaging, offering a glimpse into the world of Apple's design philosophy and technological advancements.
